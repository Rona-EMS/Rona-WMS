# Payroll Management

Manage and forecast your factory's payroll expenses. This page provides comprehensive tools for payroll management, including:

## Features

- **Payroll Forecasting**: Project future payroll costs based on current trends and planned changes
- **Department Breakdown**: View payroll costs by department to identify cost centers
- **Historical Analysis**: Track payroll trends over time to make informed decisions
- **Cost Drivers**: Understand the factors contributing to your payroll expenses

## Getting Started

1. Use the date pickers to select your desired time period
2. Switch between tabs to view different aspects of your payroll data
3. Export reports for sharing with stakeholders
4. Make informed decisions based on projected payroll costs
