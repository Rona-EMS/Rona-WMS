## What happens after you submit a report?

1. Our technical team will review your report within 24 hours
2. You'll receive an email confirmation with a tracking number
3. Updates on the resolution process will be sent to your registered email
4. Once resolved, you'll be notified of the solution and any steps you need to take

## Tips for effective problem reporting

- Be specific about what you were doing when the problem occurred
- Include screenshots or screen recordings if possible
- Note any error messages exactly as they appear
- List the steps to reproduce the issue
- Mention which browser and device you were using

## Common issues and solutions

- **Slow loading times**: Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser
- **Data not saving**: Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page
- **Display issues**: Adjust your zoom level or try a different device
- **Login problems**: Reset your password or check if your account is active
